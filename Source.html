Source records in an approval workflow and asset management automated system of a company refer to the primary documents and data inputs that initiate and fuel the processes within the system. These records serve as the foundational information for various functions, including approval requests, asset tracking, and compliance monitoring. Source records can take the form of purchase requisitions, access requests, maintenance logs, invoices, or any other documentation that triggers a workflow or pertains to asset-related activities. They are the starting point from which the automated system captures, verifies, and processes information, ensuring that each request or asset entry complies with company policies and regulatory standards. In essence, source records play a critical role in maintaining data integrity and accuracy within the system, thereby facilitating a more streamlined and accountable approach to managing assets and approvals in a modern corporate environment.
